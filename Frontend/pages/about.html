Our Story

A Space for the Art of Fashion

Grafide is a creative agency and fashion magazine brand — a space dedicated to fashion, culture, and photography. We exist to amplify the voices of creatives who are shaping the visual culture of our time.

Who We Are

Born from a deep love of fashion and visual storytelling, Grafide brings together photographers, writers, stylists, and designers under one platform. We are independent, intentional, and unapologetically creative.

From editorial features and long-form culture writing to our podcast and print magazine, everything we publish is crafted with care and a commitment to beauty in all its forms.

Our Values

Authenticity

We publish work that is honest, original, and grounded in genuine creative vision.

Craft

Every image, word, and design choice is held to the highest editorial standard.

Community

We champion emerging voices and create space for creatives at every stage of their journey.

The Grafide Design Initiative

Grafide Design is our education sub-initiative — a learning platform for graphic design, covering tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDraw, Canva, and more. Through structured courses and community mentorship, we're building the next generation of African creatives.
