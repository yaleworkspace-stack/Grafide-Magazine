Cookie Policy

This page explains what cookies we use and why.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit.

Cookies We Use

  • Authentication cookies — keep you signed in across sessions. These are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled.
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings and preferences.

We Do Not Use

  • Advertising or tracking cookies.
  • Third-party analytics that sell your data.

Managing Cookies

You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Please note that clearing authentication cookies will sign you out.
